## Demo Story

SkillProof AI turns resume claims into an auditable skill-assessment workflow:

1. Upload or paste the JD and resume.
2. Extract required skills and resume evidence.
3. Start the live skill conversation.
4. Answer targeted proficiency questions in a real back-and-forth flow.
5. Show Gemini-assisted or local natural feedback and adaptive follow-ups generated from the candidate's answer and recent context.
6. Score the conversation with the explainable rubric.
7. Show the personalized learning plan with courses, drills, time estimate, and proof artifact.
8. Show the Executive Summary: readiness, proof coverage, main risk, next action, and ROI.
9. Show the Claim-to-Proof Ledger.
10. Show ROI impact for time, cost, throughput, and accuracy.
11. Export the report.

## One-Line Pitch

SkillProof AI is not another resume chatbot; it is a claim-verification agent that maps every required skill from JD to resume evidence, targeted assessment, gap reason, proof task, learning plan, and ROI.
